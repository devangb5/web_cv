About Me

I'm a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Louisville, where I explore how defects in quantum perovskites impact materials for next-gen computing. I use a mix of electronic structure calculations, machine learning, and molecular dynamics simulations to study these effects—basically, I make atoms ‘talk’ through simulations!

Beyond research, I love hiking, exploring local spots, and discovering great food trucks—so much that I run a website about them! When I’m not outdoors, I enjoy reading or experimenting with new tech.
